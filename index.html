Description of Project 1

Hey there! So, you know how sometimes it's really hard to find your favorite games on the computer? Well, I made this super cool website that helps you find all your favorite games super duper easily! It's got these big colorful buttons that you can click on, and they take you right to the game you wanna play!

And guess what? The website is sooo fast! You don't have to wait for a long time for the games to load. They pop up right away! Oh, and it's not just for games. You can also find all your favorite cartoons and funny videos too!
